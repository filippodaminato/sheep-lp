Roadmap

Our path from product to protocol.

Phase 1 · 2025 – Build Sheep

Create a profitable enterprise tool with adaptive task networks, multi-agent orchestration, Git integration, and human review loops.

Outcome: Revenue + real-world data for STEP training.

Phase 2 · 2026 – Private Agent Network

Develop a closed ecosystem of specialized agents (code, QA, DevOps, communication). Enable internal auto-learning and contextual feedback from human teams.

Phase 3 · 2027–2028 – STEP Protocol v4

Release the open standard that allows anyone to build and monetize agents. Establish the first AI workforce marketplace powered by STEP.

Phase 4 · 2029+ – Collective Intelligence

Global network of interoperable agents capable of self-organizing, auditing one another, and co-creating products, research, and infrastructure.
