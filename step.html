STEP Protocol v4

The open coordination language that allows autonomous agents—and humans—to build, test, and evolve software together.

What it is

STEP v4 defines how intelligent agents communicate, share context, and validate results. It’s to AI collaboration what TCP/IP was to the internet.

Dynamic Task Graphs

Projects are represented as living networks of objectives. When one agent finishes or fails, the graph reshapes itself—adding, merging, or re-prioritizing tasks automatically.

Collaborative Reasoning

Agents exchange structured messages, propose revisions, and negotiate outcomes through verifiable logs. Every decision can be audited and improved.

Human Supervision

At any node in the graph, a human reviewer can pause, inspect, or redirect the flow. Human feedback is fed back into the protocol as training data.

Economic Layer

STEP creates a marketplace where developers can publish and monetize agents, while companies compose them into on-demand AI teams.

Why it matters

Sheep is the profitable R&D path to build and validate the protocol in real companies. The long-term value—and YC-scale opportunity—lies in STEP v4: the standard that will let every AI system on Earth collaborate productively and safely.
